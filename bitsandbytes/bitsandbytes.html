COMP TIA CERTIFICATIONS

Comp TIA Student Discount, Spring 2021

Students interested in taking one of the CompTIA certification exams can buy a voucher at a special discounted student rate that saves you more than 50% off the regular price.

For example, the A+ certification exam voucher, which is regularly $232, is available for just $106. Since an individual needs two exams for A+ certification, you would be able to take both for less than the regular cost of one exam.

Discounted voucher information can be found at https://academic-store.comptia.org.

If you are taking a CIT-115 course using TestOut IT Fundamentals Pro, CIT-150 using TestOut PC Pro, or CIT-220 using TestOut Linux Pro, a free TestOut certification exam attempt is included in your purchased license for this product. If you are interested, contact your instructor regarding details on how to schedule a TestOut certification exam.
